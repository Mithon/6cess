&and some alternatives

Apersands or and? Cafe or Café? If you were stuck with one of these decisions this data should help you make up your mind. Ampersands are more popular, but restaurants that use ‘and’ have higher Yelp scores. The choice is yours.

Another interesting discovery from the data is that only 4% of businesses with the word 'cafe' spell it caffe, but they have the higher average rating.
